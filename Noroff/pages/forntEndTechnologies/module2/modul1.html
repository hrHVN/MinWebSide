Lesson 1 - HTML DOM part 1

We’ll learn about the general idea and schema of HTML DOM. After this lesson, we’ll know how to find the elements we’re looking for with DOM and change their values. We’ll also add new values and remove the old ones. The last thing presented in this lesson is about changing the content of the HTML elements and their attributes. We can present our content in HTML dynamically, meaning the content will be determined when the program is run. Otherwise, our website content is predefined and static.
